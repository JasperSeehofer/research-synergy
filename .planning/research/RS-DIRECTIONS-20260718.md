# Research Synergy — Forward Proposal: Crossing the Rediscovery Ceiling (2026-07-18)

Research lead synthesis. Decides the next experiment for the cross-field LBD thread after the mechanism-reduction chapter reached its terminal state.

## 1. Where we are

We have a **validated, calibrated, unsupervised cross-field analogy engine** — the first compressed representation to beat the memory-free null on DEEP cross-vocabulary analogies where both lexical tf-idf and raw dense embedding fail (galaxy-bias↔retail-survival: lexical cosine 0.02 → reduction cosine 0.79; validated-deep recall@10 = 0.75 vs raw 0.00; cascade 0.775, MRR 0.74). A calibration control proves the bridges are **real, not confabulation** (0/80 random surface-disjoint pairs confirmed vs 5/40 reduction-selected, Fisher p=0.0035). But it is a **REDISCOVERY engine, not a novelty engine**: across n=140 and n=684 with weak-match-tail hunting, **0/12 genuine bridges were novel** to the literature. The precise gap is structural and now proven by converging evidence at two scales and two designs — retrieve-then-confirm takes the **argmax of mechanism-similarity, and that argmax IS the canonical known equivalence**, so scaling and tail-hunting cannot reach novelty. The ranking is anti-novel by construction, and the weak-match tail (ranks 4–12) is spurious, not hidden-novel.

## 2. The strategic fork

Three genuinely different bets. They are not mutually exclusive, but they buy different things and have a hard dependency.

- **Bet A — Build the ground-truth INSTRUMENT (temporal-holdout novelty benchmark).** The thread's terminal finding is not "we lack a generator" — it is "novelty has no ground truth, so we adversarially guess," and that guess already failed (0/12 via two-hunter web checks across two scales). *What it buys:* it converts "novel" from an unfalsifiable assertion into a **checkable, pre-registerable, SHA-freezable prospective number** (freeze corpus at year T, predict from pre-T reductions, grade against pairs that genuinely become co-cited in (T, T+Δ]). Crucially it is **not just measurement**: coupled with a degree-controlled preferential-attachment null and structural-hole retargeting, it ships a novelty LEVER whose success criterion — *beat the degree null on the graph-distant Poor-Poor stratum* — has an optimum that canonical rediscoveries **cannot reach by construction** (their whole appeal is high node degree). It is the precondition multiplier: it makes every Bet-B/Bet-C output testable rather than aspirational.

- **Bet B — Change the SELECTION objective (structural / off-diagonal / residue).** Stop ranking by whole-mechanism A–C cosine (which peaks at known equivalences) and instead target the novelty-complement: candidate-inference on the UN-aligned residue (structure-mapping projection), method/object asymmetric retrieval (same method, different object = a transfer), or ABC structural holes (low direct similarity, shared intermediate). *What it buys:* reaches pairs that nearest-neighbour retrieval **structurally cannot surface**, at the cost of much lower precision — heavy gating and honest grading required. Cheap; reuses the reduction, embedder, open-book card, and adjudicator almost untouched.

- **Bet C — GENERATION (generate-then-verify).** The only architecture that emits an **unstated claim**: sample a path between distant atoms no paper connects, synthesize "apply method(A) to object(B)," then verify novelty and plausibility. *What it buys:* the highest ceiling — novelty guaranteed-absent-from-corpus by construction — at the highest confabulation and integrity risk. **Un-shippable without Bet A**: a generated bridge's novelty verdict currently rests on the same noisy web-guess the project already proved unreliable, so a genuine prediction is indistinguishable from confident confabulation until the instrument exists.

**Honest hedge (not a bet against the gap):** the clean-chapter write-up (no static rep recovers cross-field analogy; LLM reduction does; calibrated but rediscovery-only; model-relative baseline; 10× enrichment control) **plus** relabelling the validated output as a **method-transfer / interdisciplinary recommender** (a known equivalence = a de-risked transfer, not a failure) is the highest-EV move if Bet A KILLs. Ship it if the instrument says novelty is unreachable.

## 3. Recommended next experiments (RANKED)

### E1 (rank 1) — Temporal-holdout degree-controlled novelty benchmark + structural-hole lever  *[attacks the gap head-on: ground truth]*
- **Hypothesis:** A genuinely novel cross-field bridge is measurable as a pre-T reduction-selected pair that becomes genuinely co-cited in (T, T+Δ] and is **NOT explained by node degree**; the current substrate carries such a signal only in the graph-distant Poor-Poor stratum.
- **Design (reusing frozen assets):** Freeze a dated OpenAlex corpus at T=2015 via `export-louvain-graph --published-before 2014-12-31` (dated citations already in SurrealDB from `bulk-ingest`). Start narrow on 2–3 targeted distant field-pairs known to have bridged (stat-physics↔ML, econophysics↔cond-mat, cosmology↔ecology/survival-stats). Emit top-K cross-field predictions from pre-T reductions only (`rs23_reduce.py` → bge embed → `rs25_cascade.py`). Grade against pairs that become genuinely co-cited (single-paper co-citation) in (T, T+Δ], **adjudicated by the frozen `rs28_adjudicate`** as real mechanistic bridges (this fixes the "Ebola↔burnout" co-mention-pollution problem). Score against a **preferential-attachment null + degree-matched surface-disjoint negatives** (AWARE protocol), stratified Poor-Poor / Rich-Rich, cross-tabbed with an Uzzi atypicality z-score. Blind-author + SHA-freeze K, Δ, the field-pair list, and the degree bins. Use rolling-origin cutoffs (multiple T) for power; Mistral-orbiter may run only the coarse "became co-cited y/n" binary at scale.
- **Prediction (pre-register):** reduction-selected predictions **beat the PA-null on the Poor-Poor stratum** at pre-registered α with ≥ N_min positives; on Rich-Rich they do **not** beat it (rediscoveries live there).
- **KILL / PASS:** KILL if reduction ranking does not beat the degree null on Poor-Poor at the powered minimum → the retrieval substrate cannot reach novelty even when measured honestly → engine confirmed rediscovery-only → ship the honest write-up + product pivot. PASS if it beats the PA-null on Poor-Poor → we own (a) a checkable novelty metric and (b) first evidence that structural-hole retargeting reaches genuine novelty.
- **Effort:** 2–3 sessions.
- **A positive result proves:** novelty is *measurable* in this thread (retires the two-hunter guess), and the graph-distant stratum carries a novelty signal degree cannot explain — the precondition that makes E2/E3/E4 falsifiable.
- **Power caveat (honest):** genuine first-time cross-field co-citations are rare; report positives-per-slice up front and treat the temporal hit-rate as a **lower bound**, never an upper bound.

### E2 (rank 2) — Candidate-inference / analogical-residue reranking  *[attacks the gap head-on: objective inversion]*
- **Hypothesis:** Ranking on the UN-aligned projectable **residue** (structure-mapping candidate inference), not on match-completeness, recovers >0 literature-novel bridges on a corpus where completeness-ranking recovered 0/12.
- **Design (reusing frozen assets):** Edit `rs22_probe_mechanism` to also emit a small relational schema (roles + relations) alongside the mechanism string — still blind, no partner shown, so the leakage-free property holds. Add a post-card **projection** step (reuse the `rs22_probe_openbook` structure): from the aligned shared card, output base relations with **no target counterpart** as candidate inferences. Rerank candidates on projectable-residue novelty/support, not reduction cosine. Gate each projected relation through the `rs29/30` two-hunter check reworded as "is this projected relation asserted anywhere in the target field?"; grade survivors temporally on E1.
- **Prediction (pre-register):** residue-ranking surfaces ≥1 genuinely novel-and-plausible bridge on a held-out slice where completeness-ranking gave 0, AND the `rs28_control` random-pair enrichment control (0/80) still holds for the residue candidate source.
- **KILL / PASS:** KILL if 0 novel over the pre-registered candidate budget AND no enrichment over random on the new source → residue ranking is not a novelty lever. PASS on ≥1 novel-and-plausible survivor that also clears E1.
- **Effort:** 1–2 sessions (runnable before E1 completes using the two-hunter interim gate; confirm on E1 once it exists).
- **A positive result proves:** changing the ranking *objective* (not the metric) escapes the rediscovery attractor the current design is mathematically trapped in.

### E3 (rank 3) — Method/object asymmetric off-diagonal retrieval  *[cheapest objective-flip]*
- **Hypothesis:** Same-method / different-object pairs (transfers) are surfaced by ranking method-atom similarity HIGH under a hard object-distance gate — matches the symmetric whole-mechanism cosine actively buries.
- **Design (reusing frozen assets):** One-field edit to `rs22_probe_mechanism` → emit `method_atom` + `object_atom`. Embed each with bge. Add an asymmetric arm to `rs25_cascade`: score = cos(method_A, method_B) under a hard gate cos(object_A, object_B) < τ AND the existing tf-idf < 0.06. τ blind-authored + SHA-frozen (a new decision constant — treat it like the others). **Validate the split quality on the `feynman_10pair` anchors first** (physics "objects" are often defined by their methods → leak risk). Feed the same `rs22_probe_openbook` card + `rs28_adjudicate` downstream; grade novel candidates on E1.
- **Prediction (pre-register):** the asymmetric arm lifts recall of same-method/different-object pairs on `feynman_10pair` vs the symmetric baseline, and on E1 contributes ≥ the symmetric arm on the Poor-Poor stratum.
- **KILL / PASS:** KILL if the method/object split is leaky on the Feynman anchors (validated first) OR gives no recall lift. PASS on a recall lift for off-diagonal pairs that survives E1.
- **Effort:** 1 session.
- **A positive result proves:** cross-TYPE (transfer) matching is a cheap, first-class objective-flip toward under-noticed transfers — a real margin gain, bounded (still only surfaces co-existing pairs).

### E4 (rank 4, GATED) — Generate-then-verify spike over a typed-atom KG  *[attacks the gap head-on: generation]*
- **Hypothesis:** A generator synthesizing "apply method(A) to object(B)" over never-co-occurring atom pairs can emit a novel-AND-plausible-AND-testable bridge that retrieval provably cannot.
- **Design (reusing frozen assets):** SciAgents pattern on existing atoms. Reduction atoms → KG nodes; add a stochastic path sampler (the only substantial new code) that selects a path between distant atoms **no paper connects**; invert `rs22_probe_openbook` to GENERATE the transfer; `rs28_adjudicate` becomes the reasoning-chain critic; `rs29/30` two-hunter becomes the novelty gate; Mistral-orbiter runs only the coarse path-triage binary (its κ 0.77–0.89 zone) — **never** the critique/ranking. **Re-run the `rs28_control` calibration in GENERATE mode** to bound the false-positive novelty rate. Grade survivors on E1.
- **Prediction (pre-register):** generate-mode calibration yields a false-positive novelty rate low enough that ≥1 generated bridge survives BOTH the two-hunter gate AND temporal confirmation; confirmation rate will be **much lower** than the 5/40 retrieval rate — report the honest hit-rate, expect it low.
- **KILL / PASS:** KILL if generate-mode calibration shows random-path generation confirmed at the same rate as targeted (no enrichment) → the generator is confabulating; or if 0 survive temporal grading. PASS on ≥1 novel-and-testable survivor clearing both gates.
- **Effort:** 3–4 sessions. **Gated:** run only if E2 or E3 shows signal AND E1 exists. Never report a generated bridge as "discovered" without the adversarial prior-art check and temporal grade; measure success as novel-AND-plausible-AND-testable, not novel alone.
- **A positive result proves:** generation crosses the ceiling retrieval cannot — the only architecture that emits an unstated claim, now with checkable ground truth behind the novelty verdict.

## 4. What I'd do first, and why

**Run E1 — the temporal-holdout degree-controlled novelty benchmark — as the next experiment.**

The binding constraint on this thread is not generation, it is **verification**. Every generative and structural bet (E2, E3, E4) produces candidates whose novelty verdict currently rests on the two-hunter web guess the project already proved unreliable (0/12 across n=140 and n=684) — meaning a genuine prediction is today **indistinguishable from a confident confabulation**, which the project's own scientific-integrity constraints forbid shipping. E1 installs exactly the missing ground truth: it is the field-standard LBD answer (Yetisgen-Yildiz & Pratt, AGATHA, Science4Cast, mat2vec), **nobody in the thread has run it**, it reuses every frozen asset plus infrastructure the repo already ships (`export-louvain-graph --published-before`, dated OpenAlex in SurrealDB, `rs28_adjudicate` as the gold-standard grader, `rs25_cascade` as the ranker under test), and it needs **no model training**. It is not merely measurement — coupled with the degree null and structural-hole retargeting it is itself a novelty lever whose success criterion (beat the PA-null on the Poor-Poor stratum) is one **canonical rediscoveries cannot meet by construction**, so it directly attacks the ceiling rather than re-finding rediscoveries more efficiently. And it yields a clean, pre-registerable, SHA-freezable **KILL or PASS in 2–3 sessions** either way: PASS unlocks E2/E3/E4 as falsifiable experiments; KILL is a decisive, honest verdict that routes us to ship the clean-chapter write-up plus the method-transfer product pivot — the highest-EV outcome under the "no over-claiming" constraint.

Sequence: **E1 first** (instrument + lever). Then grade the two cheapest objective-flips through it — **E2** (highest single-experiment novelty leverage) and **E3** (highest feasibility). Escalate to **E4** only if a signal survives, with the calibration control re-run in generate mode. Deprioritize the entire representation-sharpening family (contrastive fine-tuning, hard-negative mining, distillation, SPECTER2/SciNCL swaps): every scout and evaluator converges that sharpening the metric deepens the rediscovery attractor — a better benchmark score there is negative progress on the actual gap.
